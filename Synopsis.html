Summary of Porridge

Porridge is all about a bunch of friendly, fairy tale folk.

But in the centre of tht folk is a bunch of folk more rotten than a rancid bowl of curds and whey who are plaguing Happy Valley with crimes.

It is Jack Spratt (Private Investigator/Detective) and his new sidekick, Lucy Locket's task to find out who are plaguing Happy Valley's folk with crimes beyond the roof.

Oh, and the local cops help. Poor Seargent and Chief. they have a sightly dim crew.
